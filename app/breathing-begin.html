Simply serene
take a deep breath
in through your nose and out through your mouth
feel your body press down on the floor beneath you
Notice the weight of your body
And just Breathe
Allow the sounds around you to come and go
Notice your breath
The movement of your body
In and Out
The rising and falling sensation
In and Out
Notice how each and every breath is just a little bit different
Breathe
Realize when your mind has wondered off
And when it has bring your mind back to the breath again
Continue breathing
Let go of that thought for a few seconds
Let your mind think if it wants to think
Continue breathing
Now bring the attention back to the body
back to that feeling of weight
That feeling of contact
back to the sounds
and the smells around you
take a moment to appreciate the stillness
and remember this feeling of serenity
